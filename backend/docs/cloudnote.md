# CloudNote — Help & FAQ

## Getting Started

CloudNote is a note-taking app that syncs across all your devices. Create an account at cloudnote.example.com and download the app for iOS, Android, or use the web version.

To create your first note, click the "+" button in the top-left corner. Notes auto-save as you type. You can organize notes into folders by dragging them in the sidebar.

## Pricing

CloudNote offers three plans:

- **Free**: Up to 50 notes, 100MB storage, single device.
- **Pro ($5/month)**: Unlimited notes, 10GB storage, sync across 5 devices, markdown support.
- **Team ($12/user/month)**: Everything in Pro, plus shared workspaces, admin controls, and priority support.

All paid plans come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required to start.

## Billing & Cancellation

You can manage your subscription from Settings > Billing. To cancel, click "Cancel Plan" — your access continues until the end of the billing cycle. We do not offer partial refunds.

If you were charged incorrectly, contact support at support@cloudnote.example.com with your account email and transaction ID.

## Common Issues

### Notes not syncing
Make sure you're connected to the internet and signed into the same account on all devices. Try pulling down to refresh. If the issue persists, sign out and back in.

### Can't log in
Reset your password at cloudnote.example.com/reset. If you signed up with Google, use the "Sign in with Google" button instead of email/password.

### App crashes on startup
Update to the latest version from the App Store or Play Store. If the problem continues, clear the app cache: Settings > App > CloudNote > Clear Cache.

## Features

### Markdown Support (Pro & Team)
Write notes in markdown with live preview. Supports headings, bold, italic, code blocks, lists, and tables.

### Collaboration (Team)
Share notes with teammates. Multiple people can edit the same note in real-time. Use @mentions to notify someone.

### Export
Export notes as PDF, HTML, or plain text. Go to any note > Menu > Export.
